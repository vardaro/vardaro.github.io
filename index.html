Venndr

Mongo / Node / Express / Vue

Venndr is a web app designed to circumvent Application Tracking Systems (ATS). When a user upload their resume, Venndr extracts the keywords from their resume. It then compares the resume keywords with keywords on job postings from recruitment sites (GithubJobs, AuthenticJobs, Indeed, etc) and recommends companies whose criteria best matches the users skillset.
