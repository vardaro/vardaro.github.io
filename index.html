Biography

At a young age I expressed a keen interest in computers, mainly just playing games on them. As I got older, my enthusiam shifted toward understanding their implementation. Thus marking the onset of my journey into computer science.

By high school, I developed a job-hunting app which enables applicants to circumvent ATS software via keyword comparison. I also developed an script for online purchase automation of limited edition products capable of intelligently adjusting its path by analyzing the DOM.

In university, I've invented two esoteric programming languages (one of which receiving an award). I also wrote a tool for event organizers to environmentally optimize bus pick up locations through location clustering. For a school project, I developed NodeJS server capable of dispatching information to and from an external C++ program, thereby enabling the two languages to communicate in real time.

Currently I reside in Arlington, Texas and continue to practice computer science professionally, academically, and for my own personal enjoyment.
